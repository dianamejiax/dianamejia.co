Exalt is a program in NYC designed for and exclusively serving justice system-involved youth. Exalt inspires youth at a critical crossroads to believe in their worth, from the first steps in contemplation through the journey to create lasting behavioral change.

Some of the goals for this project were understanding internship providers’ needs, concerns, and goals, exploring how exalt could best meet those expectations, and increasing the quantity and recurrence of internship opportunities.

We did user research and were able to interview current providers and potential providers. After rounds of interviews, we conclude that providers are inspired by exalt’s core mission and are looking for information that will set them up for success in hosting interns.

Tying back to our core concept in efforts to streamline the process of communicating with their providers and gather progressive reports, we proposed a dashboard-style digital product to increase the scalability of operations.
