Info


Ve-gains highlights the advantages of maintaining a healthy vegan diet by providing daily vegan food recipes with step-by-step instructions, tips for supplementation, and nutritional bulletins issued daily.

The idea primarily evolved from personal experience and personally hearing how individuals feel about a vegan lifestyle. I wanted to create a product that could serve as a guidance tool for users interested in transitioning into a vegan diet. Entering, transitioning, or maintaining a vegan diet can be intimidating and challenging at first but with the right resources, it can be a soothing experience.

Working on Ve-gains was of great value, I learned how to create a product entirely from scratch. It started with a thought that transitioned into a process. I learned about the different stages of the development process including brand personality, design systems, wireframing, prototyping, user testing, and iterating.
