About


Hi there! My name is Diana Mejia. I am a multidisciplinary designer based in California/NY. I focus on branding, user interface/user experience, and graphic design. I enjoy creating and tackling new ideas with a curious mindset.

Most recently, I received my Master's in Communication Design at Parson's School of Design in New York City. Prior to Parsons, I graduated from California State University, Monterey Bay with honors and received my Bachelor's of Science in Communication Design along with a business minor.

A lot of my design sensibilities are influenced by my travel exposures throughout the globe and growing up in California. Wherever I am, I strive to learn and experiment new things whether it is in my travel adventures or in my design practices.
