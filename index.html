About


Hi there! My name is Diana Mejia. I also go by Di or Dee. I am a multidisciplinary designer based in California/NY. I focus on branding, user interface/user experience, and graphic design. I love the process that comes with creating a product from scratch with the goal of seeking for better solutions.

I graduated from California State University, Monterey Bay with honors and received my Bachelor's of Science in Communication Design along with a business minor in 2017. I am currently working towards my Master's in Communication Design-Digital Product Design at Parson's School of Design in New York City.

Fun fact, before committing to Communication Design, I was originally going to study Japanese language and culture because of my strong love for Japan and Japanese television shows. Although I didn't pursue it as a study, I did teach myself to read Hiragana and katakana.
