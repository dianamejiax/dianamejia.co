Project Overview: Unfold is a hypothetical conference held in Singapore. The conference explores the topic of diversity in the design space.

Roles: Branding, UI/UX, Typography, Print Design & Environmental Design

Collaborators: Sheila Cheng & Nikhil Misra
Unfold Conference

UNFOLD is a hypothetical conference made to explore ethical diversity in the design landscape and the designer's ethical role.
Developing the Brand
Open-mindedness, diversity, and the concept of 'unfolding'. These thoughts guided our design language and visual treatment for our event. We settled on on Singapore as our location. We carefully selected Singapore because of its approach for a multiracial, multireligious society.

Online Presence
To promote our event we designed a website housing information about the conference including speakers, schedule, venue and other logistics. We designed a multi-language website including English and Mandarin.

Promoting the Conference
To further promote the conference UNFOLD, we created a social media presence on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram along with an email campaign.
Our Venue
We imagined our venue to be reflective of our identity. We incorporated vibrant colors, use of our typography and combination of languages to celebrate diversity. With the goal of creating hope about a diverse future in the design world.
The Archive
After the conference concluded, our website changed appearance to recap the highlights of the event.
